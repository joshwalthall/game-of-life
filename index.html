Game of Life
What's going on here?

What you're seeing is an implementation of Conway's Game of Life , or just "Life" for short.

It's a type of cellular automata, which basically just means a grid of cells that are either alive or dead based on certain rules. For every "tick" in the cycle, each cell will either live or die depending upon how many alive or dead cells surround it.

There are many different types of cellular automata, even ones that can be simulated by hand with pen and paper. It's an interesting way to explore and think about the way that organisms grow and procreate in nature. Cellular automata have also been used for lots of interesting things such as level generation in video games.
